Hello, I'm Tulsi


I go to Yale University and I created my own major called DIGITAL AGE STUDIES.

I study the Internet and technology from socio-cultural perspectives and use that knowledge to inform my design practices. My mind lives at the intersection of technology and philosophy. I imagine a digital world that is truly creative, where imagination is consistently pushing boundaries and critical thought is integral to the process.

I'm a big fan of circles and blobby shapes. I love coffee but can't drink more than one cup a day. My favorite color right now is green. And I made this site from scratch!

This site is just a small peek into the work I've done. If you're curious to see more, visit my more informal site: moonbasil.net or email me!
