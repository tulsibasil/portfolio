Hello, I'm Tulsi

I go to Yale University and I created my own major called DIGITAL AGE STUDIES.

I study the Internet and technology from socio-cultural perspectives and use that knowledge to inform my design practices. I imagine a digital world that is truly creative, where imagination is consistently pushing boundaries.

I'm a big fan of circles and blobby shapes. I love coffee but can't drink more than one cup a day. My favorite color is green. And I have synesthesia.

This site is just a small peek into the work I've done. If you're curious to see more, visit my more informal site: moonbasil.net or email me!
